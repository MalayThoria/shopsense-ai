# Stryde Rewards Loyalty Program

Stryde Rewards is our way of thanking our loyal customers for choosing us. Every purchase you make earns you reward points that can be redeemed for discounts on future orders. The program is free to join and every Stryde customer is automatically enrolled from their first purchase.

Here is how the points system works. For every ₹10 you spend on stryde.in or at any Stryde retail store, you earn 1 Stryde Reward point. Points are credited to your account within 24 hours of order delivery, not at the time of purchase. This ensures that points are only awarded for completed orders. If you return an item, the corresponding points will be deducted from your balance.

When it comes to redeeming your points, every 100 Stryde Reward points equals a ₹10 discount on your next order. You can choose to apply your points at checkout, either partially or in full, depending on your balance. There is no minimum redemption threshold — even 10 points can be applied for a ₹1 discount. Points can be combined with sale prices and promotional discounts, though they cannot be stacked with certain limited-time coupon codes.

Our loyalty program has three tiers designed to reward our most dedicated customers. Bronze is the entry tier for all customers, where you earn the standard 1 point per ₹10 spent. Silver tier is unlocked after accumulating 1000 points in a calendar year, and it gives you a 1.5x points multiplier on every purchase. Gold tier kicks in at 3000 points in a calendar year and offers a 2x multiplier, early access to new collections, and free express shipping on every order.

To make your birthday special, Stryde Rewards members receive 50 bonus points on their birthday every year. Make sure your date of birth is updated in your profile to receive this benefit automatically.

Please note that Stryde Reward points expire after 12 months of account inactivity. Inactivity means no purchases, no point redemptions, and no engagement with the Stryde app or website during that period. To keep your points active, simply make a purchase or redeem points at least once every 12 months. You can check your current points balance and tier status anytime through the Stryde app under the "Rewards" tab or on the website in your account dashboard.
