# Stryde Product Warranty

Every Stryde product is crafted with premium materials and put through rigorous quality testing before it reaches you. We stand behind the quality of our products, which is why all Stryde footwear comes with a 6-month manufacturing defect warranty from the date of purchase. This warranty is our commitment that your Stryde sneakers are built to last.

Our warranty covers defects that arise from the manufacturing process. This includes sole separation or delamination where the outsole detaches from the upper without any external cause, stitching defects where seams come undone or unravel under normal use, zipper failures on boots or high-top models where the zipper mechanism breaks or jams, eyelet or lace loop detachment, and material peeling on synthetic uppers that occurs within the warranty period. If your product exhibits any of these issues, we will either repair the item, replace it with the same model, or issue a full refund — the resolution depends on stock availability and the nature of the defect.

There are certain conditions that are not covered under our warranty. General wear and tear, including outsole abrasion from regular walking or running, is considered normal usage and is not a manufacturing defect. Water damage, staining, or discolouration caused by exposure to moisture, chemicals, or harsh environments is not covered. Damage resulting from improper care, such as machine washing when hand wash is recommended, falls outside the warranty. Products damaged due to accidental misuse, intentional alteration, or modification are also excluded. Additionally, ordering the wrong size does not qualify as a warranty claim — please refer to our size guide before purchasing.

To file a warranty claim, email warranty@stryde.in with your order number, date of purchase, and clear photographs showing the defect from multiple angles. Our warranty team will review your claim within 3 business days and respond with a resolution. If your claim is approved, we will arrange a free pickup of the defective product and dispatch the replacement or refund within 5 business days of receiving the item. You can also visit any Stryde retail store with your purchase receipt for in-person warranty assessment.
