# Stryde Refund Process

We understand that a smooth refund experience is just as important as a great purchase experience. At Stryde, we aim to process all refunds as quickly as possible, so you are never left waiting for your money. Here is everything you need to know about how refunds work.

Once your returned item reaches our warehouse, our quality team inspects it within 24 hours to confirm that it meets our return conditions. After the inspection is complete and approved, the refund is initiated immediately. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your original payment method.

For customers who paid via UPI, refunds are typically processed and credited within 24 hours of approval. This is the fastest refund method and we recommend UPI for this reason. If you paid using a credit or debit card, the refund will be processed within 3 to 5 business days. The exact timing depends on your card issuer and their processing cycles. For payments made through net banking or direct bank transfer, please allow 5 to 7 business days for the refund to reflect in your account, as inter-bank settlement processes take slightly longer.

All refunds are credited back to the original payment method used at the time of purchase. We are unable to process refunds to a different account, card, or payment method for security reasons. If the original payment method is no longer active — for example, if your card has expired — please contact our support team and we will work with you to find an alternative resolution, which may include Stryde Store Credit.

Please note that we do not offer cash refunds under any circumstances, even for Cash on Delivery orders. COD refunds are processed via NEFT transfer to the bank account details you provide during the return process.

Partial refunds may apply in cases where the returned item shows signs of use, missing tags, or is returned without original packaging. You will be notified of any deductions before the refund is finalized. If you believe a refund deduction was made in error, you can raise a dispute through the Stryde app or by writing to refunds@stryde.in. Our team will review and respond within 48 hours.
