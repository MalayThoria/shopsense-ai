# Stryde Returns Policy

At Stryde, we want every customer to feel confident about their purchase. That is why we offer a generous 30-day return window from the date of delivery. If your sneakers, apparel, or accessories do not meet your expectations for any reason, you can initiate a return and we will make it right.

To be eligible for a return, items must be in their original condition — unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags attached. We ask that you return items in their original Stryde packaging, including the shoe box for footwear orders. If the original packaging is damaged or missing, we may still accept the return, but a small restocking fee of ₹149 may apply at our discretion.

Initiating a return is simple. You can start the process directly through the Stryde app by navigating to your order history and tapping "Request Return," or you can send an email to returns@stryde.in with your order number and reason for return. Once your return request is approved, we will arrange a free return pickup through our logistics partner Delhivery. You will receive a return label and a pickup window within 24 hours of approval. In most metro cities, pickups are scheduled within 2 business days.

There are a few exceptions to our return policy. Items purchased during clearance sales or marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged. Intimates, socks, and customised or personalised products are also non-returnable for hygiene and production reasons. Gift cards are non-refundable as well.

When processing your return, you will have the option to choose between a full refund to your original payment method or Stryde Store Credit. Store credit is issued instantly upon return approval and includes a bonus 10 percent — so a ₹2000 return becomes ₹2200 in store credit. Refunds to your payment method are processed once the returned item reaches our warehouse and passes quality inspection. You can track the status of your return through the app at any time.

If you received a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photographs, and we will arrange an immediate replacement at no additional cost.
