# Stryde Payment Methods

At Stryde, we offer a wide range of payment options to make your checkout experience as seamless as possible. Whether you prefer digital wallets, card payments, or flexible pay-later options, we have you covered.

UPI is our most popular payment method and for good reason. We accept payments through all major UPI apps including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM. UPI transactions are instant, and as mentioned in our refund policy, UPI refunds are also the fastest to process — typically within 24 hours. We support UPI payments for orders of any value with no upper limit.

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, Rupay, and American Express. Card payments are processed through our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway, ensuring your card information is always secure. For added security, all card transactions require 3D Secure authentication through your bank's OTP verification.

Net banking is available with all major Indian banks. Simply select your bank at checkout, log in to your net banking portal, and authorize the payment. This method is ideal for customers who prefer not to use cards or UPI for online transactions.

For customers who want to split their payments, we offer Stryde Pay Later in partnership with select BNPL providers. This allows you to place your order now and pay in installments over 3 to 6 months. Stryde Pay Later is available for all eligible customers and requires a one-time verification process. EMI options are available on orders above ₹2000 through select bank credit cards. EMI tenures of 3, 6, and 9 months are supported, with no-cost EMI available on select products during promotional periods.

Cash on Delivery is available for orders valued at ₹3000 or below. For security and fraud prevention purposes, we do not offer COD on higher-value orders. COD orders require a nominal convenience fee of ₹49. Please keep the exact change ready at the time of delivery, as our delivery partners may not always carry change.

All transactions on stryde.in are protected by 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your complete card details on our servers. If you face any payment-related issues, our support team is available to help resolve them promptly.
